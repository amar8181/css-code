Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) is a small woody plant native to India and North Africa. Its root and berries are used to produce a very popular Ayurvedic remedy (2Trusted Source). It’s considered an adaptogen, which means that it’s believed to help your body manage stress more effectively.
